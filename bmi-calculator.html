BMI Health Recommendations

General advice based on BMI category.

Underweight

Eat nutrient-rich foods, increase healthy calories, and consult a healthcare professional if needed.

Normal

Maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, sleep well, and stay hydrated.

Overweight

Reduce processed foods, increase physical activity, and practice portion control.

Obesity

Work with a healthcare professional on a personalized nutrition and activity plan.

BMI Calculator FAQ

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a screening tool that estimates body weight relative to height. It does not directly measure body fat.

BMI is a useful screening tool, but it does not account for factors such as muscle mass, age, or body composition.
