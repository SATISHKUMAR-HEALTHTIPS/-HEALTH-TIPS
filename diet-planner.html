⚖️ Weight Loss Diet Plan

  • 🥗 Eat more vegetables and fruits.
  • 🍞 Choose whole grains instead of refined grains.
  • 🐟 Include lean protein such as fish, chicken, beans, or tofu.
  • 🚫 Limit sugary drinks and processed foods.
  • 💧 Drink 2–3 liters of water daily.
  • 🏃 Exercise for at least 30 minutes most days.

💪 Weight Gain Diet Plan

  • 🥛 Drink milk or other nutritious beverages.
  • 🥜 Include nuts and seeds.
  • 🍌 Eat bananas, potatoes, and healthy carbohydrates.
  • 🥚 Eat protein-rich foods such as eggs and legumes.
  • 🍽️ Eat regular meals and healthy snacks.
  • 🏋️ Combine nutrition with strength training.

Diet Planner FAQ

A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats, and enough water while meeting your body's nutritional needs.

Most adults need around 2–3 liters of water daily, though individual needs vary depending on activity level and climate.

Nutrition plays a major role in weight management, but combining a balanced diet with regular physical activity is generally more effective for overall health.
