Exercise Every Day

Regular physical activity keeps your body and mind healthy.

  • 🏃 Walk for 30 minutes daily
  • 🚴 Ride a bicycle regularly
  • 🧘 Practice yoga and stretching
  • 💪 Strength training 2–3 times a week
  • ❤️ Exercise helps maintain heart health

Mental Health

Take care of your mind as much as your body.

😊 Stay Positive

Practice gratitude and maintain a positive attitude.

🧘 Meditation

Meditate for 10–15 minutes daily to reduce stress.

😴 Good Sleep

Sleep 7–9 hours every night for better mental health.
