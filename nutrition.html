Healthy Nutrition Guide

Good nutrition helps your body grow, repair itself, and stay healthy.

Healthy Food Categories

🍎 Fruits

Rich in vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and fiber.

🥦 Vegetables

Provide essential nutrients for strong immunity and good digestion.

🌾 Whole Grains

Whole grains supply energy and are a good source of dietary fiber.

🍗 Protein Foods

Beans, eggs, fish, chicken, and lentils help build muscles.

🥛 Dairy

Milk, yogurt, and cheese provide calcium for healthy bones.

🥜 Nuts & Seeds

Contain healthy fats, protein, and vitamins.

Balanced Diet Plate

Fill your plate with a variety of nutritious foods.

🥦 50% Vegetables

Leafy greens, carrots, broccoli, cabbage, cucumber, and other colorful vegetables.

🍎 25% Fruits

Choose seasonal fruits like apples, bananas, oranges, papaya, and berries.

🍗 15% Protein

Fish, eggs, chicken, beans, lentils, tofu, and other protein-rich foods.

🌾 10% Whole Grains

Brown rice, oats, whole wheat bread, quinoa, and millet.

💧 Daily Water Intake

  • ✔ Drink approximately 2–3 liters of water daily.
  • ✔ Drink more water during hot weather or exercise.
  • ✔ Limit sugary drinks whenever possible.
  • ✔ Carry a reusable water bottle.
  • ✔ Eat water-rich foods such as watermelon and cucumber.

Protein supports muscle repair, growth, and many essential body functions.

Most adults need around 2–3 liters of water daily, though individual needs vary.
